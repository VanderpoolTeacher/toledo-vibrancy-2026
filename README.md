# Toledo Vibrancy Initiative 2026 — Grant Awards Map

Interactive map of the 19 grants the City of Toledo awarded in May 2026 through the
Vibrancy Initiative, spread across 17 properties in 10 neighborhoods.

**Live site:** https://vanderpoolteacher.github.io/toledo-vibrancy-2026/

## About the grants

Mayor Wade Kapszukiewicz and Chief Growth Officer Brandon Sehlhorst announced the
2026 awards on May 15, 2026. The Vibrancy Initiative is a suite of three programs:

- **Façade Improvement Grant** — restores the exterior of a commercial building.
- **Planning Grant** — funds early architectural / engineering work to ready a
  vacant building for reuse.
- **White Box Grant** — interior buildout to make a vacant storefront
  tenant-ready.

Two properties received two grant types each, which is why 17 properties account
for 19 awards. The 2026 round drew 82 applications, distributes $630,000 in city
funding, and is expected to leverage roughly $13.5 million in private investment.

It's a single static file — open `index.html` in a browser, or serve it with
`python3 -m http.server`.
